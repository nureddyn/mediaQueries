Gon is enthusiastic and spontaneous, sometimes to his detriment. However, he's only so gung-ho about things because he sees life as being full of possibilities that he can use to grow stronger and find his father Ging.

He becomes enraged when he sees Neferpitou. He blames himself for Kite's death, thinking he was too weak and didn't leave when he should have.

Gon makes a Nen contract to get enough power to defeat Neferpitou. He sacrifices all of his Nen and puts his life on the line.
